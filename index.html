Employee Attrition Analysis Using Excel

Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data to evaluate revenue trends and identify high-performing product categories.


Key findings

  • The overall employee attrition rate was 16.12%, indicating that approximately one in six employees left the organization.
  • The Sales department recorded the highest number of attrition rate of 20.63% during the period analyzed.
  • Among the job roles, Laboratory Technicians experienced the highest turnover, accounting for 62 employee exits.
  • Employees who worked overtime showed significantly higher attrition, with 53.59% of departing employees having worked overtime, suggesting workload may contribute to turnover.
  • Attrition varied across job satisfaction levels, indicating that employee satisfaction may influence retention outcomes.
